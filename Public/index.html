In "Twelfth Night," Shakespeare famously wrote, "If music be the food of love, play on; Give me excess of it..." Ever since—and even long before—music has been an integral part of our culture. Recognizing its profound impact, it is undoubtedly worthy of deep study and appreciation. In this database, we explore and establish the intricate relationships between four key elements of music: instruments, songs (or works), genres, and musicians.
